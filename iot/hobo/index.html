Transducer MOBS

Sensor/Actuator Addon's for RPI, Arduino, MSP Launchpad, and more

What are "MOBS"?

MOBS are add-ons for development platforms which allow you easy access to multiple sensors, actuators, and other resources which may not be otherwise available on a platform. Some MOBS are designed to give your project access to environmental data, analog inputs and outputs, and control circuitry. Other MOBS are designed to be both an add-on as well as a stand alone product, meaning you can connect a battery to the module and it can act without the need of a development platform.

Supported Platforms

Most of our work focuses around the readily available Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and MSP430 Platforms; however, as the IoT and demand grows, we can expand into other platforms as well.

Raspberry Pi MOBs

The Raspberry Pi for $10-$35USD gives you access to a full Linux Computer which can natively support C and Python, along with a 40-pin Digital GPIO Bus and built in Ethernet/WiFi. For the IoT, this platform is a homerun allowing you to communicate with digital sensors as well as provide direct support for internet access without the need for additional hardware. For most hobbyists either this or the Arduino are first choices when getting into the world of Microcontrollers.
